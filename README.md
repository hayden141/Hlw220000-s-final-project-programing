So my project is a knights duty, a side stroller game where the goal is to get to the castle and fight goblins along the way, 1 goblin isn’t much of a threat but when multple see you it can be a different story. To play it uses wasd, and space bar,and the attack button is on k, and is a spear. The goblins have 2hp meaning you have to hit them twice the player doe have some i frames after being hit but just enough to where you don’t take machine gun like damage. I wanted to keep it simple with a small level and using simple shapes for the charters, as requested, and made sure to make all of these suggestions into what my game should be. Truthfully was a good call i was way over my head with my proposal

Creatively compared to other games this is not completely new, but i had to learn a lot, such as a heath bar visually displayed on the py.game, npc wander/detection, how to make the side scroller function,how to move a charter on screen, hit boxes etc. with all the other finals i had this was certainly a challenge and im glad this was scoped down as there was no way i could have made the whole proposal in the time i had. The carters I kept primitive, not just because it said to but because up till this point i had very little experience with drawing in py games,this did the trick for what the game needed.
